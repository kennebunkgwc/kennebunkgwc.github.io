About Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a national non-profit organization working to close the gender gap in technology. Through its Summer Immersion Program and Girls Who Code Clubs, the organization is leading the movement to inspire, educate, and equip young women with the computing skills to pursue 21st century opportunities. Our local Girls Who Code Club serves RSU 21 (Kennebunk, Kennebunkport, Arundel) girls in grades 6-12.
